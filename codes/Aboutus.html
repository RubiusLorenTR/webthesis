About Us

History

Alangilan Central Elementary School, located in Barangay Alangilan, Batangas City, was established in 1916 with the mission of providing accessible and quality education to the children of the community. For more than a century, the school has played a significant role in shaping the minds, character, and future of countless learners, becoming one of the most respected educational institutions in the area. Through the years, it has continuously adapted to the changing needs of education while remaining committed to its responsibility of developing well-rounded and productive citizens.

As one of the six public elementary schools under Batangas City District III, Alangilan Central Elementary School has remained steadfast in its pursuit of academic excellence and community service. The school is currently home to forty-three qualified and competent teachers who dedicate their knowledge, skills, and passion to the success of every learner. Guided by their commitment to excellence and service, these educators continue to inspire students and contribute to the growth and progress of Barangay Alangilan and its neighboring communities. Today, Alangilan Central Elementary School proudly carries forward its rich legacy while preparing future generations to meet the challenges and opportunities of an ever-changing world.

Mission and Vision

Mission

  • To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education where:
  • Students learn in a child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe, and motivating environment.
  • Teachers facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner.
  • Administrators and staff, as stewards of the institution, ensure an enabling and supportive environment for effective learning to happen.
  • Family, community, and other stakeholders are actively engaged and share responsibility for developing life-long learners.

Vision

We dream of Filipinos who passionately love their country and whose values and competencies enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to building the nation.

As a learner-centered public institution, the Department of Education continuously improves itself to better serve its stakeholders.

Core Values

  • Makadiyos (God-loving)
  • Makatao (Humane)
  • Makakalikasan (Nature-loving)
  • Makabansa (Patriotic)

Objectives and Goals

  • To provide quality education that is accessible to all learners.
  • To develop the intellectual, social, emotional, and physical potential of every learner.
  • To promote a culture of lifelong learning and continuous improvement among students, teachers, and staff.
  • To foster a sense of community and social responsibility among learners.
  • To prepare students to become responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.

Message of the Principal
